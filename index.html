Getting to Know CALABARZON


CALABARZON, also known as Region IV-A. CALABARZON is one of the most dynamic regions in the Philippines, known for its mix of fast-growing cities, rich history, and strong agricultural roots. It includes five provinces — Cavite, Laguna, Batangas, Rizal, and Quezon — each offering something unique, from industrial hubs to scenic lakes and beaches. The region plays a huge role in the country’s economy, especially in manufacturing, tourism, and agriculture. At the same time, it remains deeply connected to Filipino culture and traditions, making it a place where progress and heritage come together naturally.
